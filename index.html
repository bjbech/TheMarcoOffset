The Marco Offset

The Marco Offset is a way of calculating your minimum donation to Relay FM for St. Jude 2025 fundraiser.

The way it is calculated is you take the base model price of an iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Pro, or iPhone Air that you are purchasing during the month of September and subtract that from your total purchase price. Whatever that difference is what your minimum donation should be. This includes but is not limited to the following:

  • Phone upgrades (size, storage, etc)
  • Accessories
  • AppleCare (Monthly multiply by 12)
  • Taxes
  • Shipping costs

To help with this process a calculator has provided below. The first line is the total on your receipt. If you make monthly payments for AppleCare on the second line add what that monthly payment is.
